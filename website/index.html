The study question

Can routinely available information estimate 30-day risk—and remain calibrated in another Swedish region and a later year?

The primary model is a transparent penalised landmark regression. More complex ML must prove added value in the same untouched validation sets, not merely win on AUC.

Architecture

Separate development from proof.

People never cross partitions. Every predictor must be available before its landmark. The external region and future period stay sealed until the model and analysis are frozen.

Decision gates

A strong AUROC cannot open the door.

01

Calibration

Predicted probabilities match observed risk across region, time, and care setting.

02

Net benefit

The model improves decisions over current practice without an untenable alert burden.

03

Equity

Subgroup performance and uncertainty reveal no unresolved material harm.

04

Safety

Patients, clinicians, security, ethics, and independent oversight all approve progression.

“Research only” is not a footnote. It is the operating mode until benefit is demonstrated prospectively.

Non-negotiable

  • No automated decisions
  • No policing or sentencing
  • No denial of care
  • No public patient data or fitted weights
  • No deployment by default
